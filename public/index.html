KWATIN INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL OF SCIENCE

PRINCIPAL'S SPEECH

Two decades ago, We became fascinated with the idea of exposing kids to the future of world-class educational experience earlier than the usual. What if your kids could understand the basics of being a science student before they get to senior class? Or imagine an educational environment that expose its student to what they should expect in University ... Student shouldn't just be taught to be brilliant but help them to be intellects. Kwatin International School of Science mission is to give the students a very solid foundation and early stage exposure in Sciences, we combine the best pratice with the most current innovative approach to learning to equip your child for the future in a swiftly dynamic world. we have advanced an approach to education that balances academic, expertise, self and social intelligence, cretivity, critical thinking and digital literacy. we understand that balance amongst these scope is important in education to enable children develop to their fullest potential.

I feel privileged to lead such an exciting and vibrant school, which offers great opportunities for the young people of Nigeria. It is our goal to make each and everystudent to succeed. We offer our students the best possible opportunities to become confident, thoughtful young people who are prepared for any future challenges, in an exciting and increasingly global world. At Kwatin International School of Science, we are very proud of our caring, inclusive ethos where we set high standards and expectations for our students to achieve and behave their very best. From the moment a student joins our school community we want them to feel a sense of happiness and belonging, as this is essential to their future success. Our superb facilities and dedicated staff enables us to deliver our programmes in an environment that is intimate, friendly and attuned to every specific educational need. We keep our classes and tutorial groups relatively small to create an atmosphere that is comfortable for the teacher and student to get to know eachother.

It would be a great pleasure to welcome you to Kwatin International School of Science and we look forward to starting the development of your child together with you.

Ms. Bashir Shukurah
